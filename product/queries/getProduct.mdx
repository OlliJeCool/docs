---
title: 'getProduct'
description: 'Returns a Product fetched by its public ID, is used to access the user information from the site displayed after scanning a Product QR code.'
---
    
    ## Arguments
    
    <ParamField body="productPublicId" type="String!"/> 

    
    ## Returns

    <Info>If the `Product` does not exist, this query returns a `NotFoundException`.</Info>
    <ParamField body="getProduct" type="Product">
    Object containing information about a product.
    <Expandable title="fields">
    <ResponseField name="claimed" type="Boolean!">Indicates whether the product is claimed or not.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="imageSrc" type="String">Source URL of the product image.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="name" type="String!">Collection name.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="orderId" type="String!">ID of the order the product was bought under.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="productColor" type="Color!">Indicates the products color.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="productType" type="Type!">Indicates the product type.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="publicId" type="String!">CUID automatically assigned on creation.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="specialBackground" type="Background">When the product has a special background connected to it.</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="user" type="ProductUser">When the product it is connected this user.</ResponseField>
    </Expandable>
    </ParamField>
